overview & about project

Best 4 Years is an independent company whose main mission is outreaching to college students and faculty. Our company addresses ineffective communication between university staff and students in terms of receiving guidance, mental health support and the management of the college workload. Currently based in NJ, however there are future plans of connecting to more universities nationwide.

conclusion      

As the lead, co-project management and later on UX/UI designer, PM is definetly the career path I would like to pursue. Along the way of this journey was indeed hectic but doable. Our product is need of demand for university students not in NJ but worldwide. During this time, my team and I did fall short, however as a the lead and co-project management we did faught through it and took control. There was future plans for this project to really be influential and branch off. Overall, this project and product has create conversation for us as students and as a society. My team and I indeed helped shine light onto the topic of the student vs acadmeic gap, in addition to the needs of students while navigating college AKA the "Best 4 Years" of life.
