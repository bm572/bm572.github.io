Best 4 Years [Project Management]


Overview & About

Best 4 Years is an independent company whose main mission is outreaching to college students and faculty. Our company addresses ineffective communication between university staff and students in terms of receiving guidance, mental health support and the management of the college workload. Currently based in NJ, however there are future plans of connecting to more universities nationwide.

conclusion

As the lead, co-project management, and later on UX/UI designer, PM is definitely the career path I would like to pursue. Along the way, this journey was indeed hectic but doable. Our product is in need of demand for university students not in NJ but worldwide. During this time, my team and I did fall short; however, as a lead and co-project management, we did fight through it and took control. There were plans for this project to be influential and branch off. Overall, this project and product have created conversation for us as students and as a society. My team and I helped shine light onto the topic of the student vs academic gap, in addition to the needs of students while navigating college AKA the "Best 4 Years" of life.
